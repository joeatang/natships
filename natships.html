how it works

You are not browsing a menu. You are tuning a receiver. Every tap, click, or press moves the transmission through three phases — and each ship in the deck has its own version of all three.

step 1 · noise The signal arrives blurred and cold. The ship is still a rumor.
step 2 · lock Tap again. The image stabilizes and the vessel becomes legible.
step 3 · drift One more tap. It fades into a warm afterimage — copy the seal to keep it.
